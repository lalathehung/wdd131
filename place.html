Facts About Hong Kong

Hong Kong Culture

Hong Kong is a blend of East and West, known for its skyscrapers, bustling markets, and dim sum cuisine. Festivals like Chinese New Year and the Mid-Autumn Festival are celebrated with vibrant traditions.
